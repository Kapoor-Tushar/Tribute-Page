Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011

Steven Paul Jobs was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. He was an American enterpreneur, inventor, business magnate, media proprietor and investor. He was the co-founder, the chairman and the CEO of Apple. He was also the chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar, a member of the Walt Disney Comapny's board of directors and the founder, chairman and CEO of NeXT.
